# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We want HAWK to be a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of
background or identity.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith; disagree without hostility.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Respect privacy — no sharing of others' personal data.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code,
and may ban repeat or serious offenders. Report concerns privately to the maintainer.

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
